Chicken Broth with Kamut grain


Ingredients
  • 130g Kamut
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive
  • 350g chicken, diced
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Herbes de Provence
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red chilli flakes
  • 1.25l chicken or vegetable broth
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • Handful of coriander to serve

Method
  1. Place Kamut and 500ml of water in a medium-sized saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, and simmer covered over low heat until tender, about 50 minutes.
  3. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the chicken and heat until browned. Remove the chicken from pan and set aside.
  4. Add the onion, carrot, celery, yellow bell pepper, and salt to the pan and heat until vegetables have softened, about 6 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic and heat for 1 minute.
  6. Add the tomato paste, Herbes de Provence, chilli flakes, salt and pepper, and heat for 30 seconds.
  7. Add the broth and tomatoes to pan, bring to a simmer, and heat covered for 15 minutes.
  8. Stir in the cooked Kamut and chicken, heat for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve!
